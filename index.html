Statement on Film & Design Minor + Future Intentions

Throughout my journey at NUQ and Film & Design Minor I have taken many courses that really boosted me in storytelling not only in its traditional film format, but beyond in every other aspect of my life. Taking courses that allowed me to explore different ways of showing myself and my ideas (including 360 films, short films, posters, 3D animation, Game Design, 3D printing, and all the other means) is something I am very grateful for.

While I am actively pursuing a career in more of a product role in a tech industry, I believe Film & Design Minor and classes I took only boost my position -- after all, it is communication and storytelling skills that are most important, as in every area you can grab someone's attention by telling a cohesive and meaningful story.

Biography

I am a designer, developer, and storyteller focused on crafting emotionally impactful digital experiences. My work spans short film, game development, and product design, often centered around themes of memory, identity, and perception. I have created many films exploring guilt, perspective, forgiveness, memory, love, and connection; developed interactive games inspired by titles like Disco Elysium, and designed Eterna, an AI-powered journaling app that transforms everyday moments into structured, meaningful memories. With experience in tools such as Unity, Blender, and Figma, as well as hands-on skills of filmmaking and design, I combine visual storytelling with technical execution to build immersive, user-centered experiences. My goal is to create work that not only engages audiences but also leaves a lasting emotional and reflective impact.
